About Me

As a newcomer in the technology field, I am currently on a path to enhance my skills in various areas of information technology with enthusiasm and motivation for learning and growth. By completing courses in Python and artificial intelligence, and also learning web design and UI/UX, I am seeking an opportunity to showcase my abilities in a challenging and dynamic work environment.

I built this website with the purpose of both showcasing a project as part of my portfolio and featuring my personal information on the homepage as a resume. On other pages of this site, you will find samples of my web design projects, which will help you assess my level of proficiency in the field.
